About Me

A photographer who finds inspiration in the natural world, skin, texture, relationships, and humanity. Jordynâ€™s work stands out due to their full-immersion approach. He creates euphoric worlds of queer intimacy & fantasies, striking bodyscapes displaying the dynamics between our bodies and the earth, architecture of the body, along with touch & movement. Jordyn started taking photos as a teenager in rural Ohio, and practiced the craft all throughout middle & highschool, eventually moving to Chicago, IL in 2016 to seriously pursue his passion for the arts. Although he is now pursuing a degree in Physics, Jordyn started out with no college education, and solely relied on networking via social media and events. Jordyn released his first short film, Nightlapses, in 2021 and has been published in countless publications locally and internationally, as well as taken part in 7 galleries.
